About

Protein QuickRef is a gene research aggregation site that directly takes the user to relevant sources based on their search specifications. This site does not contain unique information. Rather, it contains references to UniProt, OMIM, DepMap, The Human Protein Atlas, cBioPortal, Pharos, IMPC, and Open Targets.

Functions: covers the various molecular and cellular functions that the protein provides (source: UniProt, OMIM)

Animal Models: presents different research studies over many years, specifically involving mice (source: OMIM)

Protein Level in Cell Lines: plotted data directly from Harvardâ€™s Quantitative Proteomics of the Cancer Cell Line Encyclopedia (source: DepMap)

Subcellular Location: diagrams of specific subcellular location of the protein, along with cellular components (source: UniProt, The Human Protein Atlas)

Tissue Distribution: tables of tissue distribution and expression in organs within the body (source: The Human Protein Atlas)

RNA expression in different cell lines: displays rna and protein expression in specific cell lines using table graphs (source: The Human Protein Atlas)

Mutation Frequency in Cancer and Survival Rate: displays data of Cancer Types Summary and comparison/survival rate using the TCGA PanCancer Atlas Studies (source: cBioPortal)

Mouse Phenotypes: phenotyping data and studies associated with mice (source: IMPC)

Drug Targets: identification and prioritisation of potential therapeutic drug targets and collection of target, disease, and ligand information (source: Pharos, Open Targets)
